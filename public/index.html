About this

Made with ❤️ and only for fun.
Play with the cube and try to resolve it.

Build with only Vanilla Javascript, FA ICONS and GSAP for some animations.

43 trillion.
Or more accurately, 43,252,003,274,489,856,000.
This is the number of possible configurations that can occur in the Rubik's cube.

Erno Rubik made the Rubik's Cube on May 19, 1974.
The cube is made of 27 pieces or small cubes, and it has 6 faces (each of a different color).
